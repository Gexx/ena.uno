Help

  • In this game you will need a lot of concentration and fast reactions.
  • On top of the screen you can see all players sitting at the table.
  • Yellow is you, Green is player who is playing.

Game Moto

  • Speed And Efficiency
  • Speed speed speed!

Game Rules

  • The first player who have zero cards in hand wins the game.
  • Valid card is the one that matches color or symbol, or if it is a Wild card.
  • You can't finish the game with a Wild Card, if you try to do so, you will draw 2 cards.
  • Every game starts clockwise from dealer, unless first card on bottom is Rotate card.
  • You can decide to draw even if you have valid card to play.
  • You can skip players if you have card with the same symbol and color as the card on table.
  • When playing zero, you can switch cards with another player. (Use this power wisely.)

Game Dynamic

  • Game starts when table is full, or if 2 or more players are waiting for more than 20 seconds.
  • At the begining of the game, 108 ENA cards deck is dealt to the table.
  • Each player gets 7 cards.
  • Top card is face up dealt to the table center.
  • Dealer is choosen randomly between all the players at the table.
  • Dealer starts the game, unless first card on bottom is Skip card.
  • You have 15 seconds to react, or else you will draw 1 card automaticly.

Provably Fair

  • The game is Provably Fair, meaning that site can mathematicaly prove that it does not cheat on you.
